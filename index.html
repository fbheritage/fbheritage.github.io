FB Heritage

FB Heritage was set up by Freya Bohea in 2013 to provide high quality services within the heritage sector including research, recording, analysis and the conservation management of heritage assets.

Services include:

  • The production of Conservation Management Plans for historic buildings and historic landscapes.
  • Historic building surveys.
  • Historic garden / historic landscape surveys.
  • Heritage Statements / Heritage Impact Assessments.
  • Listed building consent applications / other planning applications involving heritage assets.

Freya has been passionately involved and working in the heritage sector for many years. She has a BSc in Heritage Conservation, a MSc in the Conservation of Historic Buildings and is a member of the Institute of Historic Building Conservation.

Clients include:

  • The National Trust
  • Private owners
