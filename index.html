FB Heritage Conservation (Freya Bohea)

FB Heritage Conservation was set up by Freya Bohea (formerly Bowles) in 2013 to provide high quality research, recording and conservation management services to the heritage sector.

Services include:

  • Research and production of Conservation Management Plans/Conservation Statements for historic buildings and historic landscapes
  • Research and analysis on historic buildings and historic landscapes
  • Measured surveys and historic building recording
  • Historic garden/landscape surveys
  • Heritage statements

Freya has been passionately involved and working in the heritage sector for many years, has a BSc in Heritage Conservation, a MSc in the Conservation of Historic Buildings and is an Affiliate member of the Institute of Historic Building Conservation.

Clients include:

  • The National Trust
  • Private owners
