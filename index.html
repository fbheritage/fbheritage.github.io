FB Heritage Conservation (Freya Bohea)

FB Heritage Conservation was set up by Freya Bohea (formerly Bowles) in 2013 to provide high quality services to the heritage sector.

Services include

  • Conservation Management Plans / Conservation Statements
  • Research and analysis on historic buildings and historic landscapes
  • Historic building recording
  • Heritage Statements to support Listed Building Consent applications
  • Planning appeals
  • Marketing Plans for heritage sites

Freya has been passionately involved in the heritage sector for many years and has a BSc in Heritage Conservation and an MSc in the Conservation of Historic Buildings.
